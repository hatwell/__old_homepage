About Me

I am a project manager and front end web developer based in Western Massachusetts. My most recent role was managing the production and deployment of Drupal websites, and training clients to administrate their websites.

I have a BA in American Studies from King's College London, and attended the University of North Carolina. During my career, I have worked at companies including Experian and Google, and with clients including IKEA, GlaxoSmithKline and Unilever.

My areas of expertise include Drupal, live events and conferences, the education sector, and iOS.

In my free time I enjoy cycling, weight lifting, meeting animals, watching Seinfeld and drinking IPAs.
